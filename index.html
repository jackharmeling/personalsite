My Favorite Game

I'm Jack Harmeling, a second-year Data Science at Northeastern University. Some of my hobbies are reading, playing basketball, listening to music, hanging out with my friends, and watching sports. I'm interested in machine learning and predictive models, and for a recent project I predicted NBA MVP voting using ML models (somewhat succesfully). I'm from San Diego, California and have three dogs. Something I'm passionate about is my favorite video game, Football Manager. Football Manager puts you in the shoes of the Manager position of any soccer club in the world - you're in charge of managing finances, signing players, setting up training, keeping players and the board happy, among a number of other tasks. The game is very data-driven, and different skins and databases can be downloaded in order to further analyze player and financial data in order to further your team's success. The game makes loads of data available on every subject you could need - player/manager/club skill, personality, history, stats, etc. Analysis is an extremely important aspect of the game, as using player data to make the best signings and tactics is the only way to succeed without getting sacked. Football Manager was the original reason I wanted to pursue Data Science as a major, and thus I thought would be appropriate to make this website about.
